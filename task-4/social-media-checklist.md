# Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

**Name:** Uttam Pankajbhai Savaliya\
**Reg No:** 25BAS10005

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## ✅ DO's

-   Use strong and secure passwords\
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)\
-   Keep your personal information private\
-   Verify information before sharing\
-   Be respectful and polite online

## ❌ DON'Ts

-   Do not share sensitive personal details (OTP, passwords, bank info)\
-   Avoid posting inappropriate or offensive content\
-   Do not click on suspicious or unknown links\
-   Avoid interacting with unknown or untrusted people\
-   Do not engage in cyberbullying
